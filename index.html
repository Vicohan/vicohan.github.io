Biography [CV]

I am currently a third year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Pheng-Ann Heng and Prof. Chi-Wing Fu. Previously, I received the B. Eng degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology in Zhejiang University in 2015, under the supervision of Prof. Deng Cai.

My research interest includes deep learning for 3D understanding, medical image computing, and deep learning for point cloud analysis.
